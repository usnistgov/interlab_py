Chemometric Outlier Detection and Classification of 2D-NMR Spectra
==================================================================

.. currentmodule:: mab_utils

This is the landing page for the use of Interlab on the data from the NISTmAb Interlaboratory Study. The analysis posted here can also be found at `our publication in Chemometrics and Intelligent Laboratory Systems <https://doi.org/10.1016/j.chemolab.2020.103973>`_. Below, you can find links to the data processing page and the three cases discussed in the paper.
